## What it's Nessus?

**WPScan** is an open-source tool made with Ruby code that serves us to recognize, enumerate information, and discover vulnerabilities for websites made with Wordpress. It is accessible with `MacOS` and `UNIX` devices.

>If you want to get the tool and learn a bit about it's information, you can go to this [**Github repository**](https://github.com/wpscanteam/wpscan/wiki/WPScan-User-Documentation).
{: .prompt-info }

### Create GA account and property

First, you need to set up your account on Google analytics. While you create your account, you must create your first **Property** as well.

1. Head to <https://analytics.google.com/> and click on **Start Measuring**
2. Enter your desired _Account Name_ and choose the desired checkboxes
